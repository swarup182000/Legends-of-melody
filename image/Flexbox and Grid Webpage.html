Legends of melody

Honey Singh

Yo Yo Honey Singh is a popular Indian rapper, singer, and music producer known for revolutionizing Indian music with his catchy beats and fusion of hip-hop and desi styles. Famous for tracks like "Brown Rang" and "Lungi Dance," he has become a trendsetter in the Indian music industry.

Sonu Nigam

Sonu Nigam is a legendary Indian playback singer celebrated for his melodious voice and remarkable versatility across languages and genres. With iconic songs like "Kal Ho Naa Ho" and "Abhi Mujh Mein Kahin," he has left an indelible mark on the Indian music industry.

Shreya Ghoshal

Shreya Ghoshal is a highly acclaimed Indian playback singer known for her melodious voice and exceptional versatility. With timeless hits like "Teri Ore" and "Sun Saathiya," she has won numerous awards and is a beloved figure in the world of music.

B Praak

B Praak is an Indian singer, music director, and composer known for his powerful vocals and emotionally stirring songs. Gaining fame with hits like "Teri Mitti" and "Filhall," he has become a prominent figure in Punjabi and Bollywood music.

Falguni Pathak

Falguni Pathak is a celebrated Indian singer and performer, known as the "Dandiya Queen" for her iconic Garba and Navratri songs. With timeless hits like "Maine Payal Hai Chhankai" and "Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi," she has charmed audiences with her melodious voice and vibrant stage presence.

Rahat Fateh Ali Khan

Rahat Fateh Ali Khan is a legendary Pakistani singer, renowned for his soul-stirring renditions of qawwalis, ghazals, and Bollywood songs. A disciple of the iconic Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, his hits like "Tere Mast Mast Do Nain" and "Afreen Afreen" have earned him global acclaim.
